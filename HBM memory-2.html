Quantum Basics

Qubits differ from classical bits by existing in probabilistic superposition (0 AND 1 until measured), with entanglement enabling exponential compute power:

  • 300 entangled qubits: 2^300 possible states (more than atoms in the observable universe)
  • Decoherence: Qubit fragility (heat/vibration) → Main technical hurdle
  • Hybrid Computing: Nvidia's Icing/NVQLink enables GPU-QPU error correction and calibration
